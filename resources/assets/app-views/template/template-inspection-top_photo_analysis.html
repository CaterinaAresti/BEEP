Top Photo Aanalysis protocol (EN)

  1. Blow a puff of smoke into the hive from below.
  2. After a minute, remove the lid and take first a high-resolution photo from the label of the hive and then of the topside. For the accuracy of the photo analysis, take care to include the entire top frame in the photo, and use a standard angle (35mm) (Figure 1).
